FitnessHub Privacy Policy Last Updated: 13 November 2023 Welcome to FitnessHub! This Privacy Policy outlines how FitnessHub collects, uses, maintains, and protects information obtained from users of the FitnessHub website.
1. Information We Collect 1.1 Personal Information • User Registration: When users sign up for an account on FitnessHub, we may collect personal information such as name, email address, and other relevant details required for account creation. • Billing Information: For users making purchases through FitnessHub, we may collect billing information, including credit card details and billing address, to process transactions securely. Non-Personal Information • Usage Information: We may collect non-personal information about how users interact with FitnessHub, including pages visited, duration of visit, and other statistical data. • Device Information: FitnessHub may collect information about the device used to access our website, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
2. How We Use Collected Information FitnessHub may use collected information for the following purposes: • Personalization: To personalize user experience and deliver content and offerings relevant to individual preferences. • Transaction Processing: To process transactions, including purchases of products or services through FitnessHub. • Improvement: To improve our website based on user feedback and usage patterns. • Communication: To send periodic emails and updates related to FitnessHub, including promotions, newsletters, and account-related information.
3. Protection of User Information FitnessHub employs industry-standard security measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and FitnessHub cannot guarantee absolute security.
4. Sharing of Information FitnessHub does not sell, trade, or rent user information to third parties. However, we may share aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal information with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.
5. Third-Party Websites Users may find advertising or other content on FitnessHub that links to the sites and services of our partners, suppliers, advertisers.
